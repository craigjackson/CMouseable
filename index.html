The demo's purpose is to showcase the capabilities of the CMouseable plugin for jQuery. CMouseable integrates mouse actions with the HTML canvas tag with just a few lines needed to start and tons of customizable options.

Right now, you can click to select a box or drag rectangles to select multiple boxes. Once you have selected at least one box, click and hold down inside a selected box to begin dragging the selected box(es). You can use the shift key to add to your selection, as well.

Some of the customizations are:

  • Set the draggable area within the canvas area.
  • Extend CMouseableRect to modify colors, modify shapes, modify anything with the object.
  • The area that accepts the mouse inputs (canvas, body, window, etc).

Future plans:

  • Ability to interface with the CMouseable plugin.
  • Ability to use Ctrl, Alt and Shift keys to adjust your mouse commands.

The 4 Rendering States of CMouseable

Normal State - Medium Grey
Nothing in happening to the box.
Selecting State - Light Grey
Click in an open area and drag over the box to see the selecting state. This tells you that the box will be selected if you use this selection area.
Selected State - Dark Grey
Follow the selecting state instructions above. Then, let go of the mouse button to see the selected boxes in the selected state.
Dragging State - Medium Grey with Transparency
Once you have at least one box selected. Click inside one of the selected boxes to beginning dragging. You cannot move the boxes out of bounds.
